Chicken Salad

Description

A delicious and healthy chicken salad recipe perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup celery, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup grapes, halved (optional)
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, mayonnaise, celery, red onion, and lemon juice.
  2. Mix well until all ingredients are combined.
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. If desired, fold in grapes and walnuts for added flavor and texture.
  5. Serve chilled on a bed of lettuce or as a sandwich filling.
